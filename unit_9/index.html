Hello, I'm Marta

I'm a designer raised in Chicago, born in Poland. Ever since I could remember, I always loved exploring new things. Discovery and learning always excited me—I loved school, learning English, and the American culture. I've always been the type to roll up my sleeves and jump right in.

With the thirst for knowledge tugging at me, I recently decided to get into digital design. I came across General Assembly and signed right up for class. I created this site for my class project; completely coded and designed by me. I wanted to design a simple responsive portfolio page with a clean asthetic and intuitive navigation and one that works fluidly on various devices. I'm amped on this constant-evolving digital side of design and love that I can keep learning and building up this new found skillset. I'll keep adding examples of my digital work to this page as I build content as a Visual Designer.
